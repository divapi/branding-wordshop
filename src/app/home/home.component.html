Imagine Dragons

American pop rock band formed in 2008 in Las Vegas, USA. They became famous after the release of their debut studio album Night Visions in September 2012. American Billboard magazine named them the brightest new stars of 2013 and the biggest band of 2017, and Rolling Stone magazine named their single "Radioactive" the biggest rock hit of the year.
