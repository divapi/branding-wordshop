Imagine Dragons - Thunder

"Thunder" - is a song by American indie rock band Imagine Dragons, the second single from their third studio album Evolve. The song was released under Interscope Records. It peaked at number 4 on the Billboard Hot 100 and became the country's fourth single, ranked in the top ten. It also made the top 10 in Australia, Austria, Estonia, Canada, Germany, Italy, New Zealand, Poland, Slovakia, Slovenia, Sweden. The song received a Grammy nomination for Best Pop Duo/Group Performance.
